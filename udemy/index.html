Empowering Careers Changers: Let's travel to the world of programming!


Posted on Wednesday, November 15th 2023 by Iana Accioly

In this article we will read about the fundamental languages of the web, i.e HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

This article will be dived in 4 sections:

  1. Where to start?
  2. What is HTML?
  3. What is CSS?
  4. What is JavaScript?

Where to start?

To start, HTML is the best foundation. It's simpler and helps you understand the structure. You'll learn about tags, which are like commands that tell the browser what to show. Next, dive into CSS. It's like giving life to your HTML. Learn how to style your webpage, change colors, fonts, and make it visually appealing. Finally, get into JavaScript. Start with basic things like alerts or changing text on a click. Then explore functions, loops, and events to make your webpage more interactive. There are plenty of online tutorials, YouTube videos, and interactive platforms like Codecademy or freeCodeCamp. Start small, practice a lot, and you'll soon create your own amazing webpages!

What is HTML??

HTML is like the skeleton of a webpage. It structures the content, like text, images, and links. Imagine it as the framework that holds everything together.

What is CSS?

CSS is the stylist of the webpage. It decides how things look, like colors, fonts, and layout. It's like the painter that makes the webpage beautiful.

What is JavaScript?

JavaScript is the magician! It adds interactivity and makes things move. You can create pop-ups, games, and dynamic elements. It's like the brain behind the actions on a webpage.
